Portfolio

Project 1: Canting Website

Canting is a website (capable of stunting) which contains information related to stunting and can monitor the development of children and pregnant women which focuses on the Mojokerto area.

Project 2: STICK HERO 4 GAME

Stick Hero is a simple game where the gameplay involves the player double-clicking the mouse to extend a stick to help the hero cross blocks, with the score increasing each time the stick is successfully used. If the hero falls, the game ends and must be restarted from the beginning. There are nine characters to choose from, and the game sound can be adjusted through an icon in the top left corner of the screen.

Project 3: UI/UX Prototype E-Commerce SCT

This is a UI/UX design concept for a secondhand goods e-commerce app. What sets it apart from other apps is the inclusion of two unique features: a barter option, allowing users to trade items based on mutual agreement, and an auction feature for bidding on items.
